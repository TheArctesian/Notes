## Area
- Parallelogram
	$$ A =bh$$
	Where b = base and h = height
- Triangle
	$$ A = \frac12(bh) $$
	Where b = base and h = height
- Trapezoid
	$$ A = \frac12(a+b)h$$
	a,b = parallel sides h = height

- Circle
	$$ A = \pi r^2$$
	r = radius

- Cylinder Curve
	$$ A = 2\pi rh$$
	r = radius h = height

## Volume
- Cuboid
	$$ V = lwh $$
	l = length, w = width, h = height
- Cylinder
	$$ V = \pi r^2 h $$
	r = radius, h = height
- Prism
	$$ V = Ah $$
	A = cross-section area, h = height
