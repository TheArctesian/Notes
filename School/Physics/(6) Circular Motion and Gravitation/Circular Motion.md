$$
w = \frac{\Delta\theta}{\Delta t} \; in \;\frac{rad}{s} \; or \; rad \times s^{-1}
$$

$$
v = rw
$$

$$
a = rw^2 = \frac{v^2}{r} = \frac{4\pi^2r}{T^2}
$$

$$
